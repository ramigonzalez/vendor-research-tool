## Story
**As a** user,
**I want** to browse previous research runs,
**so that** I can revisit results without re-running the expensive pipeline.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Job history section (collapsible) visible on page load.
2. On load: fetches `GET /api/jobs` and renders list of jobs.
3. Each job entry shows: creation timestamp (formatted), status badge (running/completed/failed), "View Results" button (disabled for failed/running).
4. "View Results" fetches `GET /api/research/{job_id}` and renders full results in place of matrix.
5. Empty state message: "No previous research runs. Click 'Run Research' to start."
6. Failed jobs show with red status badge and no "View Results" button.
7. "Could Have" — cut if time-constrained.
