## Dev Notes
**Depends on**: Story 7.4.

**Client-side formulas** (from `scoring.ts`):
- Source Count: `min(evidence.length, 5) / 5`
- Authority: mean of `AUTHORITY_WEIGHTS[source_type]`
- Recency: mean of recency scores (<=180d=1.0, 180-365d=0.7, >365d=0.3)
- Consistency: `supporting_count / total_count`

**Maintenance note**: `scoring.ts` header documents that it mirrors `engine.py`. Any scoring changes must update both files.
